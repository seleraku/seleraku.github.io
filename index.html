Welcome to Seleraku

Seleraku brings you indonesia's most vibrant and colorful cuisins,from jakarta's local modest shops to street-side snacks and top dollar plates. Here you can find all of your favorite indonesian dishes like nasi goreng ,sate ,bakso and our freshly home-baked sweets like martabak-manis,kue putu ayu and more. All dishes are prepared by local indonesian Chefs using only specially chosen ingredients,including imported herbs ,spices and sauces.
